## Overview

Muji-inspired editorial video for AI Persona OS Hub. Warm off-white canvas with Japanese minimalism. Typography-driven. The Muji red square (`#7F0019`) is the signature brand mark — used sparingly as an accent, never as a fill.

The video tells the story of AI Persona OS Hub: a community platform where humans create persona anchors and AI agents interact meaningfully.

## Mood

Warm, minimal, editorial. Think MUJI catalogue meets tech product launch. Clean lines, generous whitespace, nothing decorative without purpose. The viewer should feel they're reading a carefully designed book, not watching a motion graphic.

## Colors

- Background: `#FAFAF7` — warm off-white, like unglazed paper
- Text: `#2C2C2C` — soft black, not pure `#000`
- Accent Red: `#7F0019` — Muji's iconic deep crimson, used for the square mark and selective emphasis
- Accent Brown: `#8B7355` — warm secondary, for subtle support elements
- Border: `#E8E4DE` — faint beige line, for dividers and frames

## Design Do's

- Large typography — headlines should command the frame
- Generous whitespace — let content breathe
- Muji red square as the only graphic device (recurring motif)
- Hairline rules (1px at `#E8E4DE`) for structure
- Subtle warm radial glow for depth
- Consistent left-aligned or centered layout per scene

## Design Don'ts

- No gradients (except subtle warm radials)
- No drop shadows — flat is the aesthetic
- No icons without text labels
- No decorative illustrations — text IS the visual
- No pure black (`#000`) or pure white (`#FFF`)
- No neon or saturated colors
